iPhone 14 Pro Max

Meet Dynamic Island.

Dynamic Island bubbles up music, sports scores, phone calls, and so much more — without taking you away from what you’re doing. You can touch and hold to control your music, view the battery life of your AirPods, and more. It’s integrated throughout iOS — and works with lots of different apps — to seamlessly surface what you need, right when you need it. The advanced quad-pixel sensor on the Main camera makes the most of 48 megapixels by adapting to what you’re shooting.
